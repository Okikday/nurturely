Nurturely is a Flutter-based mobile application dedicated to supporting maternal health and safe delivery. It empowers mothers with comprehensive resources, financial planning tools, and a supportive social community.

Key Features
• AI Health Assistant: An integrated chatbot providing personalized guidance on nutrition, exercise, and general health inquiries.
• Daily Advice: Personalized, bite-sized daily tips to support mothers through every stage of pregnancy and neonatal care.
• Financial Planning: A built-in savings tool to help families budget effectively for childbirth and baby-related expenses.
• Community Hub: A dedicated space for mothers to connect, share experiences, and provide mutual support.
